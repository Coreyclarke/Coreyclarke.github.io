What Is Net Neutraility

Net Neutaility is something enforced by the FCC to keep all types of traffic within the internet to have the same priority as any other piece of information traveling throught the internet, this allows no throttling of certain services or any throttling of your internet, unless they tell you that your internet is being throttled

This also allows people to start up their own web pages or services and not have to compete with other huge internet corporations who have the ability to pay more for internet speed, while others that could have potentual do not
